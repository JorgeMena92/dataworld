# References

A curated list of people, blogs, books, and learning platforms worth following. Not exhaustive — just the ones that consistently deliver value for anyone growing in the data space.

## 🏢 Companies Worth Studying

Not for job hunting — for learning how mature data cultures operate and make decisions.

| Company | Why It's Worth Studying |
|---------|------------------------|
| [Google](https://research.google/) | Strong data culture, experimentation mindset, and open research |
| [Netflix](https://netflixtechblog.com/) | Data-driven decision making, A/B testing at scale, recommendation systems |
| [Amazon](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/big-data/) | Metrics-obsessed, customer-centric, pioneers of the modern data stack |
| [Microsoft](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/data-architecture-blog/bg-p/DataArchitectureBlog) | BI tools, enterprise data solutions, Fabric and Azure ecosystem |
| [Airbnb](https://medium.com/airbnb-engineering) | Product analytics, marketplace optimization, strong engineering blog |
| [Databricks](https://www.databricks.com/blog) | Delta Lake, Lakehouse architecture, platform best practices |

## 👤 People to Follow

### Power BI, DAX & Fabric

- [**Guy in a Cube** (Adam Saxton & Patrick LeBlanc)](https://www.youtube.com/@GuyInACube) — the go-to Power BI YouTube channel, consistent and practical
- [**SqlBi** (Marco Russo & Alberto Ferrari)](https://www.sqlbi.com/) — the most technically rigorous DAX and data modeling content available
- [**Avi Singh (PowerBIPro)**](https://www.youtube.com/@PowerBIPro) — Power BI tutorials with a focus on real-world scenarios
- [**Pragmatic Works**](https://www.youtube.com/@PragmaticWorks) — Power BI, Fabric, and Azure deep dives
- [**Will Thompson**](https://www.youtube.com/@WillThompson_BI) — focused Fabric content as the platform evolves

### Databricks & Data Engineering

- [**Zack Wilson (DataExpert.io)**](https://www.dataexpert.io/) — data engineering career advice, pipelines, and practical project walkthroughs
- [**Andreas Walmsley**](https://www.youtube.com/@andymarkham) — Databricks, Delta Lake, and data engineering patterns
- [**freeCodeCamp.org**](https://www.youtube.com/@freecodecamp) — full beginner courses across SQL, Python, and data analysis

### SQL & Analytics

- [**Alex The Analyst**](https://www.youtube.com/@AlexTheAnalyst) — beginner-friendly SQL projects and portfolio guidance
- [**Luke Barousse**](https://www.youtube.com/@LukeBarousse) — SQL for analytics and job preparation
- [**Ken Jee**](https://www.youtube.com/@KenJee_ds) — data career advice and data science explanations
- [**Tina Huang**](https://www.youtube.com/@TinaHuang1) — data career insights and productivity

## 📚 Books

| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|------|--------|-------------|
| [The Data Warehouse Toolkit](https://www.kimballgroup.com/data-warehouse-business-intelligence-resources/books/data-warehouse-dw-toolkit/) | Ralph Kimball | The definitive guide to dimensional modeling — essential for anyone building BI solutions |
| [Storytelling with Data](https://www.storytellingwithdata.com/books) | Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic | How to design charts and communicate data clearly to non-technical audiences |
| [Fundamentals of Data Engineering](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/fundamentals-of-data/9781098108298/) | Joe Reis & Matt Housley | Modern data engineering stack from ingestion to serving — practical and up to date |
